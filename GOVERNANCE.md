# Governance · P7CO® EcoResupply Blog

This document describes how decisions are made and responsibilities are distributed in the **P7CO Blog** repository.

---

## 👥 Roles

### Core Team (pH7x Systems)
- Maintains the repository and has merge rights.  
- Defines the vision, mission, and editorial line of the project.  
- Reviews and approves Pull Requests.  
- Ensures compliance with licensing, Code of Conduct, and contribution rules.

### Contributors
- Anyone who submits Issues, Pull Requests, or Translations.  
- Do not have direct merge rights.  
- Contributions are reviewed by the Core Team before integration.

### Translators
- Volunteers who translate content into supported languages (`/en/`, `/pt/`, `/fr/`, `/de/`).  
- Must respect terminology and structure defined in `CONTRIBUTING.md`.  
- PRs go through review by the Core Team or assigned language reviewers.

---

## 🔄 Decision Process

- **Pull Requests (PRs)**  
  - All PRs are reviewed by at least one Core Team member.  
  - Approval requires:  
    - Compliance with `CONTRIBUTING.md`.  
    - Respect for the `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`.  
    - Correct license attribution.  

- **Issues**  
  - Anyone may open Issues (bugs, content suggestions, translation requests).  
  - Core Team triages Issues and assigns labels (`bug`, `enhancement`, `translation`).  

- **Releases**  
  - Content updates are continuous (merged PRs appear in the next site deployment).  
  - Governance rules are updated when necessary by consensus of the Core Team.

---

## 🌍 Community Involvement

We encourage contributions from:  
- Translators (PT, EN, FR, DE to start).  
- Reviewers who validate content clarity and factual accuracy.  
- Community members suggesting new articles or improvements.
